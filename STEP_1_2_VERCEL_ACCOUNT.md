## üìù Step-by-Step Instructions

### Option A: If You DON'T Have a Vercel Account

**1. Go to Vercel:**
   - Open your web browser
   - Go to: **https://vercel.com**

**2. Sign Up:**
   - Click the **"Sign Up"** button (top right)
   - Choose one of these options:
     - **GitHub** (recommended - easiest)
     - **GitLab**
     - **Bitbucket**
     - **Email** (if you don't use Git)

**3. If Using GitHub:**
   - Click "Continue with GitHub"
   - Authorize Vercel to access your GitHub
   - Vercel will create your account automatically

**4. If Using Email:**
   - Enter your email address
   - Check your email for verification link
   - Click the link to verify
   - Create a password
   - Complete signup

**5. After Signup:**
   - You'll be taken to the Vercel dashboard
   - You should see "Add New Project" or "Import Project"

---

### Option B: If You ALREADY Have a Vercel Account

**1. Go to Vercel:**
   - Open: **https://vercel.com**
   - Click **"Log In"** (top right)
   - Sign in with your account

**2. Go to Dashboard:**
   - You should see your dashboard
   - Look for **"Add New Project"** or **"Import Project"** button
